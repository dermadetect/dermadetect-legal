Delete Your DermaDetect Account

Last updated: April 19, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your DermaDetect account and the data associated with it. DermaDetect is developed by DermaDetect Ltd.

How to Request Deletion

To delete your account and all associated personal data, send an email to developer@dermadetect.com with the following:

  1. Subject line: Account Deletion Request
  2. The email address you used to register your DermaDetect account
  3. (Optional) a note indicating whether you would like us to delete your case data only, or your full account and all associated data
Verification For your protection, we will send a confirmation to the email address on file before processing the deletion. Requests from other email addresses will not be honored.

What Gets Deleted

Upon successful verification, the following data is permanently deleted within 30 days:

  • Your account profile (name, email, date of birth, gender)
  • All skin condition photographs you have uploaded
  • All symptom questionnaire responses
  • All AI-generated preliminary assessments
  • All specialist review notes associated with your cases
  • Push notification tokens and device identifiers
  • Authentication tokens and session data

What We May Retain

In limited circumstances, we may retain certain information after account deletion:

  • De-identified, aggregated data used to improve our AI models — this data cannot be linked back to you
  • Records required by law (for example, audit logs or financial records) — retained only as long as required by applicable law, typically up to 7 years
  • Backup archives — your data may persist in encrypted backup archives for up to 90 days before being purged on our standard rotation schedule

Processing Time

We will acknowledge your request within 5 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days of verification. You will receive a final email confirmation once the deletion is complete.

Questions or Concerns

If you have questions about the deletion process or your data, contact us at developer@dermadetect.com.

See our Privacy Policy for more information about how we handle your data.
