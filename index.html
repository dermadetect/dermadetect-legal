DermaDetect Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

DermaDetect ("we", "us", "our") operates the DermaDetect mobile application. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal and health information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, date of birth, gender
  • Health information: photographs of skin conditions, symptom questionnaire responses
  • Assessment data: AI-generated preliminary assessments, specialist review notes
  • Device information: push notification tokens, device platform (iOS/Android)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered skin condition assessments
  • To enable specialist review of your cases
  • To send you notifications when your case has been reviewed
  • To maintain your medical file and assessment history
  • To improve the accuracy of our AI models (using de-identified, aggregated data only)

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Authentication tokens are stored in secure device storage
  • Server-side data is stored in encrypted databases
  • Photos are stored in secure cloud storage with access controls

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Your case data is accessible only to:

  • You (through the app)
  • Reviewing dermatologists assigned to your case
  • DermaDetect system administrators (for support purposes)

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Your data is retained for as long as your account is active
  • You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at support@dermadetect.com
  • Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data

Medical Disclaimer

DermaDetect provides preliminary AI-assisted assessments for informational purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical concerns.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.

Contact Us

For questions about this policy or your data, contact us at support@dermadetect.com.
